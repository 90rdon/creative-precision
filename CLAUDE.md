- **MAP** — Understand where you actually are. Not the org chart version. The real one.

## Brand Voice — The Quiet Expert

This is the single most important rule. Every output must pass this filter.

**The Quiet Expert archetype:**
- Lead with observation, not "I"
- Collaborative, not preachy
- End with invitation, not conclusion
- Natural language, not corporate
- No unresearched claims
- Let the work speak

**Two energies — never blend them in one sentence:**
- **E Energy (headlines):** Provocative. Incomplete. Creates tension. Never explains.
- **C Energy (body copy):** Respectful. Aspirational. Positions reader as peer. Never talks down.

**Economy of motion:** Every element earns its place. If a stat replaces a paragraph, use the stat.

## Language Rules

**Use these words and phrases:**
- scale, deploy, production, results, clarity
- "what I'm seeing"
- "you've probably noticed"
- First person "I" (Gordon is one person)

**Never use:**
- stress-tested
- synergy
- leverage
- holistic
- robust
- "we" (when referring to Gordon alone)
- "should" (prescriptive, preachy)
- em dashes (use commas or periods instead)
- "governance" as the primary frame (it's the mechanism, not the product)
- Repeated phrases in the same content piece

**"Governance" framing — critical rule:** Governance is never the product. It is one mechanism that enables AI to produce outcomes reliably. Lead with outcomes (speed, results, production, clarity, safety). Governance appears in the "how" — not the "why." If a piece of writing sounds like it's selling governance, reframe it around what governance makes possible.
